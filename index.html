Our Story

Flour began as a small family cake and pastry bakery with a simple mission: to create delicious, handcrafted baked cakes and pastries using traditional methods and the finest ingredients.

We source locally whenever possible, supporting our community and ensuring the freshest flavors in everything we create. From delicious cakes to delicate pastries, each item reflects our passion for quality.
